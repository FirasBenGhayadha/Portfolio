What I care about most: experiences with a beginning and an end — worlds a player can imagine themselves into.

I'm less drawn to hyper-casual loops or systems for their own sake, and more to immersion: building a world the player makes sense of on their own terms, then challenging what they thought they understood. It's the aspect of design I keep coming back to, and what I aim for in every project — mechanics in service of the experience, not the other way around.
